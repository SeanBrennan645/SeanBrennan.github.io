Castle Perpetually A Looping Castle

LDJAM47

This was my teams entry for the Ludum Dare 47 game jam. Our team consisted of old university friends looking to get back into some game development since finishing our course. The theme for the jam was Stuck in a Loop.

As it had been some time since most of us had done some game development our overall goal was to produce a finished game regardless of size. We collectively agreed to do a 2D game and each design a room with a puzzle to unlock the doors. The catch being that one of the doors would send the player back to the start of the game.

During this project I got to work on some scripts as well as assisted those in the team who weren't as familiar with unity as the rest of us were. In the end we were able to successfully reach our goal of creating a finished game.
