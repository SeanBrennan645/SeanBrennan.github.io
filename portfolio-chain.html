Chain Shot

A short complete game

This game was my entry for the Mini Jame Gam #9 hosted on itch.io using Unity

The point of this jam was to complete a game regardless of size which was perfect due to the limited time I had the weekend we had to make it. The theme was there is only one with a special item listed as a chain.

For my entry I went with the concept of only having one shot where you lose if you miss. Armed with your trusty chainsaw gun you must fend off the unending hordes of angry logs. This game is available on my itch page right now!
