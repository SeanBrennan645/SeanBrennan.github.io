Second Year Uni Project Chilly No Mates

A Unity Game

This game was created as part of my second year professionalism module. It primarily gave myself and my teammates experience in using Github for source control as well as being our first time using the Unity engine.

I primarily helped to work on scripts related to picking up and using items as well as helping my team present the game at numerous points during the development of the game.

The concept of this game was that the player had blacked out the night before while out clubbing and had to find key items such as their clothes, phone and wallet before heading back to the club for another night out.
