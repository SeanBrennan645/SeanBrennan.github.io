Humble Beginnings

Jungle Root was the first game I had ever created. Paired with 2 other students we had only a few weeks to develop and present our first ever game.

We used the Stencyl tool to develop our little 2D platformer adventure and were able to have different enemies, far too difficult platforming challenges and even a final boss!

While this game is far from the most impressive game I have ever worked on I still take a great deal of pride in it being the first one I worked on. We even uploaded the game to Newgrounds for people to play where it is still available at time of writing this page.
