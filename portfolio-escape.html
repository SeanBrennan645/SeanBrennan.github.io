Try to Escape

GGJ 2021

Escape was a small game created as part of the Global Game Jam 2021 Cultural Heritage Jam using the Unity engine.

The game takes inspiration from Japan with the gameplay focused around a simple puzzle box based on a Japanese puzzle box. The player must rotate the box in order 1-6 in order to solve the puzzle and exit the room.

The game had the setup for more functionality and was planned to be much bigger however due to other priorities among the teams personal lives we were happy to at least make something small yet complete.

A link to the game can be found on my itch.io page linked with my other socials at the bottom of this webpage.
